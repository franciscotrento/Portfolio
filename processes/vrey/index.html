I'm vrey into you

After meeting in Montréal, two creatures wanted to keep meeting and writing from the germ that was activated last summer. Kathy Acker and McKenzie Wark’s I’m Very Into You fabulated into I’m Vrey Into You. Vrey, as said after one returns from the Red Room in Twin Peaks, where words have another temporality. This resonated with our bodies in different chronological times zones while still living in a spectropoetic togetherness. Soon we couldn’t stop working in vrey, and now, 7 weeks after saying “See you soon!“ there are already 100 pages. Writing together, loosing both our -and finding many more-than - voices made us think about non-typicality, creating conditions, and anarchival techniques which will hopefully attune to conversations of a future-past.

Francisco Trento and Anouk Hoogendoorn
