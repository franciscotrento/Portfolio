I'm vrey into you
After meeting in Montréal, two creatures wanted to keep meeting and writing from the germ that was activated last summer. Kathy Acker and McKenzie Wark’s I’m Very Into You fabulated into I’m Vrey Into You. Vrey, as said after one returns from the Red Room in Twin Peaks, where words have another temporality. This resonated with our bodies in different chronological times zones while still living in a spectropoetic togetherness.
